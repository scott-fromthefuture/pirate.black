Got Gas?

To make transactions on the BSC network you must first have a small amount of BNB for gas in your wallet. BNB is available on many of the exchanges that have also listed ARRR.


Wherever you decide to get it, be sure to get the BEP-20 version that goes to an address that starts with "0x", and not BEP-2 version for addresses that start "BNB".

wARRR Staking Guide

STEP 1: Provide Liquidity

The liquidity pool on pancakeswap is a 50/50 pool meaning if you lock an equal amount of wARRR and BNB (in USD value) in the contract, you will earn a proportion of the transaction fees in poportion to your share of the liquidity pool. To get BNB, you can exchange wARRR for some on PancakeSwap

Before begining its important to understand the risks by reading the complete staking guide below.


Add Liquidity on PancakeSwap:
ADD BNB & wARRR Remove BNB & wARRR

STEP 2: Stake wARRR-LP

Once liquidity's provided, you will receive wARRR-LP tokens that represent your Liquidity Position.


Stake these wARRR-LP to begin earning your portion of the fees!
